Our Menu

Chicken

Yami Restaurant's chicken dishes are a symphony of flavors that tantalize the taste buds and leave a lasting impression. Their secret lies in the meticulous preparation, where fresh, high-quality chicken is expertly marinated in a blend of aromatic spices and herbs. The result is a tender, juicy, and flavorful chicken that melts in your mouth. From the classic General Tso's Chicken, with its crispy exterior and sweet and sour sauce, to the fiery Kung Pao Chicken, boasting a tantalizing blend of spicy chilies and peanuts, Yami's chicken offerings are a testament to their culinary prowess. Their Szechuan Chicken, with its bold and spicy Sichuan peppercorns, awakens the senses with a tingling warmth. The Honey Garlic Chicken, glazed with a sweet and savory sauce, provides a delightful balance of flavors, while the Lemon Chicken, infused with a bright and tangy lemon sauce, offers a refreshing twist. Each dish is meticulously prepared with the freshest ingredients, ensuring a burst of flavor in every bite. The chicken is cooked to perfection, maintaining its juicy tenderness while boasting a crispy exterior, creating a delightful textural contrast. Accompanying these culinary masterpieces are a medley of fresh vegetables, perfectly stir-fried to retain their vibrant colors and crunch. From crisp broccoli and tender mushrooms to vibrant bell peppers and juicy onions, the vegetables complement the chicken perfectly, adding depth and complexity to each dish. Yami Restaurant's chicken dishes are not merely a meal, but an experience that delights the senses and leaves you yearning for more. Whether you prefer a classic or a more adventurous flavor profile, Yami's chicken offerings are sure to satisfy even the most discerning palate.

Beef

Yami Restaurant's beef dishes are a testament to the artistry of Chinese cuisine, showcasing the rich, savory flavors of this beloved ingredient. Their chefs meticulously select high-quality cuts of beef, ensuring tenderness and marbling that melts in your mouth. Each dish is prepared with a unique blend of spices and sauces, creating an explosion of flavors that tantalize the taste buds. The classic Beef and Broccoli, a comforting and satisfying dish, features tender beef strips stir-fried with crisp broccoli florets in a savory sauce. The Kung Pao Beef, a fiery and flavorful option, combines tender beef cubes with peanuts, chilies, and a spicy sauce, creating a tantalizing symphony of textures and flavors. For those seeking a more subtle approach, the Beef with Ginger and Scallions offers a delicate balance of savory and fragrant flavors, with tender beef slices infused with the warmth of ginger and the freshness of scallions. Yami's beef dishes are not only a culinary delight but also a testament to their commitment to quality ingredients and meticulous preparation. Whether you're a fan of bold flavors or prefer a more subtle approach, Yami Restaurant offers a range of beef dishes that are sure to satisfy your cravings..
